Olive the Movie is the first full length feature film shot completely on a smart phone. A 35 mm lens adapter was fabricated to fit the smart phone in order to achieve a shallow depth of field. The film stars two time Academy Award nominated actress Gena Rowlands. The film simply put is about a little girl that goes into the lives of 3 people, and old lonely woman, a foreigner having a hard time acclimating in the United States, and an obese man… and transforms their lives without saying one word!

The financing of Olive was done independently of any corporation. The goal now is to be the first film to not only be shot on a cell phone but to also be the 1st independently financed feature film on 2000 theaters across the United States without the backing of a Major Studio.
